The Frontline Health Workers Coalition is an alliance of United States-based organizations working together to urge greater and more strategic U.S. investment in frontline health workers in developing countries as a cost-effective way to save lives and foster a healthier, safer and more prosperous world. The Coalition encourages the U.S. government to help train and deploy these frontline health workers as part of a comprehensive health workforce strengthening strategy, and to invest in countries that have severe health worker shortages and in which the U.S. is already making a substantial investment to improve health through its Global Health Initiative.

www.frontlinehealthworkers.org

Contribution: The Frontline Health Workers Coalition will distribute engagement opportunities to their 30+ members’ organizations, including the possibility to test, evaluate and scale up programs. It also will promote the partnership with USG and global stakeholders.

The MDG Health Alliance is comprised of eminent leaders from the private, public, and nonprofit sectors working in conjunction with the Health and Education Cluster of the Secretary General's MDG Advocates to support country efforts to accelerate progress toward achieving the health-related Millennium Development Goals (MDGs 4, 5 and 6). The MDG Health Alliance was initiated and is led by Ray Chambers, MDG Advocate and United Nations Secretary General's Special Envoy for Financing the Health Millennium Development Goals and for Malaria. The Alliance seeks to fulfill the vision articulated in the UN Secretary-General's Every Woman Every Child movement by coordinating private-public partnerships in support of the UN and broader efforts.

www.mdgha.org

Contribution: The MDG Health Alliance is contributing to country-scale up and is a co-chair of the Country Programs Task Force.

 

The mHealth Alliance leverages the game-changing potential of mobile technology in pursuit of transformed health outcomes for billions of the world’s most vulnerable people. To this end, the mHealth Alliance brings together experts from every side- health, government, technology, and development- to spark new connections and drive collective action for mainstreaming mHealth. The Alliance powers Health Unbound (HUB), a global online interactive network and knowledge resource center, and serves as secretariat to two innovative partnerships, the Mobile Alliance for Maternal Action (MAMA) and mPowering Frontline Health Workers.

www.mhealthalliance.org/

Contribution: Through its Health Unbound platform and networks, the mHealth Alliance will support the sharing and dissemination of reports, research findings and other papers produced as a result of mPowering Frontline Health Worker activities. The Alliance will also play a role in the partnership’s communications and advocacy strategy

The One Million Community Health Workers (CHWs) Campaign is devoted to catalyzing the rapid training, real-time management, and large-scale deployment of frontline workers like CHWs throughout sub-Saharan Africa. The Campaign builds upon the successful advocacy and awareness efforts of the global community over the past years, in the name of the Millennium Development Goals, to mobilize financial and technical advisory for the upgrade and expansion of national CHW programs. CHWs, particularly when enabled with mobile technologies, are a crucial delivery mechanism for expanding the reach of primary healthcare systems, making them the strategic solution to reducing morbidity and averting mortality in mothers, newborns and children. The Campaign is operated by a small secretariat based in New York with field staff in Africa, and has engaged 10 countries in its first year - with more to come!

1millionhealthworkers.org
